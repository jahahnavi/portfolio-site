Malle Pulu
← Home

Malle Pulu is a hypthetical film that showcases my way of romanticising Bangalore and showing the city through a young woman’s eyes — the little moments and everyday beauty that often go unnoticed.

It began with my love for the city and the question — what is it like to be new here and see the city for the first time?

Read Full Script Here

This script captures a single morning in Bengaluru through a woman's eyes — poetic, mundane, and quietly radical. We see her speak through choices, silences, and routines rather than words.

The moodboard brings together textures, tones, and cultural references that shaped the world of Malle Pulu — soft browns, old cottons, jasmine strings, and Ray-like framing.
